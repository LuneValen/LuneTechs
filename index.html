Why LuneTechs?

I have explored many sources of learning materials, especially those that students use to study subject topics or prepare for exams, and I have noticed that most of them are in English. This is mainly because English is an international language, which allows it to reach people all over the world. This is indeed true and can be clearly observed in our daily lives.

However, even though English is an international language, it does not mean that everyone can speak or read it—especially those who have not had the opportunity to attend school. Nowadays, information and communication technology is developing so rapidly that we all need to keep up with the pace to cope with the current state of the world.

Having realized this, I decided to create this platform to help people, especially Tanzanians, learn more easily through the Kiswahili language, which is widely used in Tanzania. I am confident that this platform will empower everyone, including those who are not familiar with English, to build a strong foundation in ICT.
Why? Because this is the only place where you will find tutorials provided in the Kiswahili language. You are all welcome to this website — learn the easy way!
